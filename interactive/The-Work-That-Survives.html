The Work That Survives01 · The floor rises
The premise

When execution gets cheap, the floor rises.

Competent work product is now fast and close to free. As that floor rises, the work just above it stops being where a lawyer earns the seat. What stays above the line is judgment, taste, and the duty to answer for the result.

The Work That Survives02 · Three kinds of work
Click a layer

Legal work is not one thing. It is three.

CommodityOver the loop
CraftOn the loop
CustodyIn the loop

Commodity is high volume with one defensible answer. You wrote the standard, turned it on, and sample the output. You were not there for any one document, and you can still answer for all of them.

The line between commodity and craft is not fixed. It moves left as work settles, and you move up to the next frontier.
